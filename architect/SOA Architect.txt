SOA: 一种架构类型和风格
Service Oriented Architecture
Service-based development and the outcomes of services
Integrate distributed, separately maintained and deployed software components

Service Properties:
- Self-contained, inner workings is hidden
- Business activity with a specified outcome
- Composed of other services
